## Description
Implement the missing whole-program, ANF, VM CFG, ARC, and Wasm-local optimizations exposed by the Coroutine Scheduler Explore report. The current 161-line fixture produces 178 LoisVM functions, 39 instance globals, 136 closure environments, 199 indirect calls or tail calls, 373 explicit ARC operations, and about 604 KiB of WAT.

## Design
Keep each optimization at the highest IR that still exposes the semantic facts required to prove it correct:

- executable formation owns selected-entry and initializer-root semantics;
- effect-aware Buslane optimization runs before residual effect erasure;
- ANF owns administrative expression cleanup;
- VM CFG owns control flow, direct-call conversion, closure escape analysis, consuming projection selection, and slot coalescing before ARC insertion;
- VM CFG finalization owns ARC insertion and local ARC cleanup;
- the Wasm compiler owns only Wasm-specific function-table and code-generation cleanup.

Do not add a compiler optimizer to the independent LoisVM bytecode package and do not rely on Wasmoon JIT optimization to recover high-level dictionary or closure semantics.
