## Description
The formatter needs a policy layer that classifies gap-indexed trivia into leading, trailing, and detached trivia without storing trivia arrays on tokens or AST nodes.

## Design
Introduce a formatter-local `TriviaView` over concrete syntax. The view should preserve trailing comments as trailing on the syntax unit they followed, preserve meaningful blank lines as grouping trivia, canonicalize repeated blank lines, and treat EOF as a non-printing final trivia anchor.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] `TriviaView` queries trivia by token or token gap without duplicating trivia facts.
- [x] Trailing comments remain trailing when formatting groups break.
- [x] Meaningful blank lines are preserved but repeated blank lines are canonicalized.
- [x] Formatter helpers can consume trivia without adding comment fields to AST nodes.
