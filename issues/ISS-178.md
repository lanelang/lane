## Description
Resolve the apparent mismatch between a source resumption returning the handler result and a dictionary field continuation returning its CPS answer, especially when a handler leaves outer effects and resumes more than once.

## Design
At an `Install` with result `H` and residual effect row `F`, use the Church-encoded residual computation `M_F<H>` as the handled body's local CPS answer. The handled dictionary fields therefore receive reusable continuations returning `M_F<H>`, while relay dictionaries turn residual operations into suspended `M_F<H>` computations. Running the resulting computation against the outer contexts and continuation eliminates the handler.
